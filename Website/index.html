FAQ

Explore Rare and Collectible Fine Watches

What is RK Auction?

RK Auction is a premier online platform dedicated to luxury watch auctions. We specialize in rare, vintage, and collectible timepieces from renowned brands worldwide. Our platform provides a secure and transparent environment for buyers and sellers to trade exceptional watches. With years of experience in the industry, we ensure authenticity and quality for every transaction.

How do I participate in an auction?

Participating in our auctions is simple! First, create an account on our platform. Once registered, you can browse active auctions, place bids, and track your favorite items. We recommend setting up your payment method in advance. All bids are binding, and winning bidders will be notified immediately after the auction closes. Our customer service team is available 24/7 to assist with any questions during the bidding process.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept a wide range of payment methods to ensure convenient transactions for our global clientele. This includes major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, bank wire transfers, and cryptocurrency. All payments are processed through secure channels, and we offer buyer protection for every purchase. For high-value transactions, we can also arrange escrow services upon request.
